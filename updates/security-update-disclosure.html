Security Update

December 2, 2020

‍

MetaMask mobile Version 1.0

Version 1.0 has new features, including stronger encryption of your private keys. Update to version 1.0+, and make sure you have created a password.

If you are still using MetaMask Mobile in beta, we strongly suggest you update to MetaMask Mobile V 1.0.4+.

‍Remember, never share your private key or seed phrase to anyone claiming to be MetaMask support. We will never ask you to send your private keys or seed phrase to us.‍

Safety of your wallets remains our top priority. If you have questions, you can contact us at security@metamask.io.
