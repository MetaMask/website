OVERVIEW

The Widest Range of Top-Tier Crypto Custody Solutions

Custody plays a fundamental role for organizations seeking to access crypto and DeFi. Depending on the range of services offered by a qualified custodian or custody technology providerthey may cover everything from institution-compliant key storage and interaction with broker dealers and exchanges, to multi-signature transaction approval and signing. They are paramount to organizations safely acquiring and holding crypto assets.

MetaMask Institutional is the only multi-custodial institutional Web3 wallet. We partner with multiple custodians so that our clients can choose from a wide variety of solutions that meet all variations of institutional-grade custody requirements.
