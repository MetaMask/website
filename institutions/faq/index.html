MetaMask Institutional Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to commonly asked questions

Overview

How does MetaMask Institutional (MMI) differ from MetaMask?

MMI differs from MetaMask because it offers institution-required digital asset custody integrations alongside monitoring and reporting tools on the portfolio dashboard.

Is there a minimum AUM requirement to use MetaMask Institutional?

We have no minimum AUM requirement to use MMI. There might be qualifying criteria or considerations that are Custody partner specific. We strive to serve every organization on the planet as they make their foray to Web3.

How do I get started with MetaMask Institutional?

To get started, request access and, if needed, schedule a time with one of our sales team members. You can also download the brochure for more information.

Does MetaMask Institutional work with DAOs?

Yes. Many custodians are able to support DAOs. In addition, MMI has recently announced a partnership with Safe, a popular option for DAOs.

How much does MetaMask Institutional cost?

Metamask Institutional has a seat-based subscription model. Our seat-based pricing is divided across three organizations, with a maximum number of seats in each organization.

Please request access to find out more.

Further cost structures are determined by the custodian you choose to work. We recommend working directly with your custodian to determine these costs.

How long is this subscription for?

This agreement shall begin on the Effective Date and shall continue until terminated by either party.

How long does it take to onboard from start to finish?

Onboarding with MMI typically takes 2 to 3 business days.

Your custodian might have separate onboarding processes that may need to be completed before they can activate your access.

How do I offboard from my existing custodian to a new custodian?

By signing up to a new custodian, creating new accounts, importing those accounts into MMI and moving all your assets from your previous custodial accounts to your new custodial accounts.

Can I import my old custodial accounts into my new custodian?

Every custodian or custody tech provider generates keys or key shares in different ways. This means key material generated in one custodian is not necessarily importable to a new custodian. Many custodians and custody tech providers do allow you to export your private keys or key shares. Hence, providing the capability to self-custody if needed. This key material can then be used to sign transactions. However, this key material won’t be able to be imported into another custodian to custody tech-provider.

MMI Portfolio Dashboard

How do I add accounts to my organisation within the Portfolio Dashboard?

There are three ways to add accounts into the portfolio dashboard. Firstly when you transact with custodial accounts, we automatically detect and add these accounts to your organization. When you add new accounts into the Extension that you have not yet transacted with nor are part of your organization, we provide a helpful notice that we have detected new accounts not yet part of your organization and ask if you want to add them to your organization. Lastly, you can navigate to the Portfolio Dashboard’s accounts section and add any accounts you would like to your organization.

What networks do you support within the Portfolio Dashboard?

You can add addresses from any EVM Network along with Solana addresses. We support token balances across all EVM networks and Solana. We also support DeFi positions, Staking and NFTs across all EVMs. These balances will be shown in the main portfolio holdings page along with portfolio snapshots. For Transaction History, we support the following networks:

  • Mainnet, Polygon, BSC, Avalanche, Fantom, Cronos, Arbitrum, Optimism, Heco, Moonbean, Moonriver, Aurora, Celo, Base, Linea
How many members can I add to the Portfolio Dashboard and what roles can be assigned?

We have no limit to the number of members you can add to an organization.

How many different roles are available, and what can they do?

We have two roles within the MMI Portfolio Dashboard, Admin and Regular user roles. Admin users manage payments and can invite and delete users. Regular users will be invited to the dashboard. Admin users can create settings that apply at an organization-level. For example, how accounts are grouped at an organization level.

How many admin roles can one account have? What if I’d like to have multiple admins as there are a few key decision makers within the organization. How will this work? And will they have rights to override each other?

You can have unlimited admins in an organization.

Will the role setting in MMI impact my current governance settings with my custodian partner(s)?

Our current role settings do not impact any policy or governance settings at your custodian.

When you add new accounts into the Extension that you have not yet transacted with nor are part of your organization, we provide a helpful notice that we have detected new accounts not yet part of your organization and ask if you want to add them to your organization. Lastly, you can navigate to the Portfolio Dashboard’s accounts section and add any accounts you would like to your organization.

How many bundles can I create within the Portfolio Dashboard?

There are no limits to the number of bundles you can create.

I don't use the Portfolio Dashboard much/at all, will it impact trade execution using the MMI browser extension?

You will still be able to use the MMI browser extension unencumbered. In order to onboard to MMI, invite Team members and give them access to the MMI Extension, organizations have to onboard the Portfolio Dashboard. As all invoicing occurs via the Portfolio Dashboard access is also important.

How does MMI support Centralized Exchange Data support?

We work with MeshConnect to allow users to connect their Prime accounts. A user only needs their API keys and relevant password and secrets (available from your Centralised Exchange account). Account balances are brought into the token section of the portfolio dashboard.

Are Centralized Exchange Data balances added to snapshots?

Yes, snapshot valuations will include positions held on connected Centralised Exchange Accounts.

What are Snapshots?

Our Snapshots feature allows a user to set the exact time when they want to have valuations run on all the accounts connected to their organization. Our snapshots features will provide valuations on Tokens, DeFi positions, Staking Positions and NFTs. We provide this data in a read-only UI that can be shared with third parties as well as a CSV download. Please see our blog post here.

How does MMI work with my fund administrator, auditor, and tax consultant?

MMI offers Portfolio Snapshots that generates a read-only view of your organization’s holdings and transactions over a given timeframe. You can download the snapshot data from the dashboard into a .csv file or share a link with fund admins, LPs, or other third-parties to highlight valuation and transaction history. All historical on chain transactions since inception can also be downloaded in csv file from the Transaction Snapshots feature.

Does MMI do any P&L calculations especially for DeFi positions?

Yes this is now available with the Portfolio Dashboard home screen. You can choose to view P&L or performance attribution for your DeFi positions.

How does Swaps in MMI differ from UniSwap or 1Inch?

MetaMask Swaps sources liquidity from the widest sources in the market, including Uniswap, AirSwap, 0x API, 1inch.exchange, Paraswap, and dex.ag.

We aim to provide the best quote in the market at all times, and we implement technical innovations to severely limit failure rates.

MMI Extension

Which networks does MMI support?

Similar to MetaMask, MMI supports all EVM chains. As a caveat, custodial account chain support is in part determined by the custodian you choose to work with. In January 2022, MMI launched custodian account multi-chain capabilities—unlocking custodian account access to all EVM-compatible L2s and sidechains, which means MMI supports custodians in being able to have multi-chain access.

Which tokens does MMI Support?

MMI supports ERC-20, ERC-721, and ERC-1155 token types, just as MetaMask does. However, access to specific tokens are determined by your custodian’s token support framework.

Can I use MetaMask Institutional without working with a custodian?

Yes, you have the option to use MetaMask Institutional (MMI) in a self-hosted manner and with hardware wallets supported by MetaMask. Onboarding with a custodian partner is optional.

How do I connect my custodian account?

Within your custodian’s platform/interface there will be a simple button to connect your custodian account with MMI. You can then select from the list of wallets you have created in your custodian account within MMI.

APIs

What functionalities do your APIs offer?

Our APIs provide a comprehensive suite of endpoints that support operations such as taking snapshots, generating transaction histories, and retrieving profit and loss (P&L) along with performance data for accounts on Ethereum and Solana. We prioritize high reliability and performance, delivering minimal latency and maintaining a robust uptime of 99.99% to ensure uninterrupted service.

What is the pricing for your API services?

Our base tier is available at a cost of $99 and can be added to your existing MMI subscription. For standalone use of our APIs with enhanced quota limits, please contact us for more details.

Is it possible to exceed my quota limit on API requests?

Yes, you can exceed the defined quota. We apply an overage fee for any requests that surpass your quota limit. Please contact us for more details.

How and when am I billed for the API usage?

Billing occurs at the end of each month. We will send you an invoice that includes the total number of API requests made during the month and charges for any additional MMI services utilized.

Managing my Digital Assets

Can multiple users transact on the same wallet?

Yes, MMI allows organizations to filter and connect any account in the wallet from their custodian. This means users on different browser extensions can access the same wallets and accounts within.

How are my transactions sent to Mainnet?

After a transaction is confirmed in the extension, transaction details are sent to the custodian, where it can be approved and signed. The transaction is then broadcast to Mainnet from the custodian account.

How do I receive transaction status updates from MMI?

The MMI SDK receives status updates from the custodian via a webhook so that transaction status and transaction activity are updated within the extension.

Will all my token holdings in my custody account be reflected within my MetaMask Institutional wallet?

MMI reflects all on-chain transactions, including the tokens of those transactions, within the wallet. You may need to Import token contracts into your MMI wallet.

Does MetaMask Institutional provide any insurance coverage in the case of private keys being compromised or hacked?

MMI does not hold any private keys on behalf of the customer. Private key management is done at the custodian level, hence the custodian you choose to work with is responsible for private key security and recovery. Different custodians have different recovery protocols and insurance policies, best to check with your chosen custodian.

Who can I contact if I experience any issues?

Our Customer Support team consists of experienced technical support engineers combined with a self-service online helpdesk platform that provides the current status and full history of each support request. Please refer to the customer support language outlined in the Subscription agreement.
