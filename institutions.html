MetaMask for Institutions

Enterprise-grade security, operational and reporting features for Institutional Finance—built into the world's most trusted Ethereum wallet.

Get in touch

Why MetaMask for Institutions?

The Ethereum DeFi ecosystem has reached $22B in collateral. Cryptocurrency funds, custodians, and professional traders increasingly seek exposure and access to the diverse decentralized finance opportunities that compose Web3.

MetaMask for Institutions will allow businesses to swap tokens, borrow, lend, invest, and interact with DeFi protocols and applications using the familiar MetaMask interface—upgraded to include enterprise security, operation, and reporting features to power a professional DeFi trading platform.

Institutional Features

Enterprise Security

Simplify capital deployment into DeFi applications with multi-signature support and direct integration with the leading custody providers.

Risk Management & Ops

Leverage network data to inform capital allocation decisions, and integrate configurable approval flows to trigger more sophisticated trades.

Reliable Trading

Eliminate transaction front running with mempool monitoring and private transaction submission. Support BLS key generation and withdrawals for Eth2.

Robust Compliance

Ensure compliance across process flows and accurate reporting.

The Early
Adopter Program

Prior to the commercial launch of MetaMask for Institutions, ConsenSys will collaborate with a select group of partners, custodians, and professional trading firms. Program participants will be able to shape product features, influence the development roadmap, and gain early access to this integration for their own clients.

Ideal partners have customers already deploying capital into DeFi protocols. They will be closely engaged during the course of product development and will give feedback throughout beta testing.

Program Benefits:

  • Brand Visibility: Feature in a joint press release announcing the Early Adopter Program. Leverage the MetaMask brand as the leading DeFi wallet extension.
  • White-Glove Treatment: Work directly with the product team to influence early features and roadmap. Ensure the product meets your needs before our commercial launch.
  • Competitive Advantage: Move quickly to differentiate from competitors and offer early access to your clients.
