Buy cryptocurrency with MetaMask

Buying cryptocurrency with MetaMask has never been easier. Just click on the “Buy” button on the MetaMask browser extension or mobile app to get started. You can also connect your wallet to MetaMask Portfolio to get started.

The improved user experience shows you the best quotes based on your region, your preferred payment method, the token you want to buy, and the providers that are available to you.

With a funded MetaMask wallet, you’re ready to explore Web3 on your own terms.

Buy across networks

You can buy stablecoins and native tokens from within MetaMask globally across a wide variety of networks: Ethereum, Polygon, Binance Smart Chain, Avalanche, Fantom, Celo, Optimism and Arbitrum.

Own your crypto in a private and secure way

Privacy-preserving

Any personal information you provide during the purchase is not shared with us: MetaMask doesn’t store or process payment details, personal data, email addresses, etc.

Freedom-preserving

Unlike a centralized exchange, neither our partners nor MetaMask have access to the cryptocurrency after you purchase or transfer. As a result, users can immediately use their assets without a holding period.

Vetted providers

MetaMask ensures that all providers comply with applicable regulations and that they are legitimate, responsible parties.
